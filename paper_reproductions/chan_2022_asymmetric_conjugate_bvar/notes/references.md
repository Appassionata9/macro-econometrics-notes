# Chan (2022) References

## How to Use This List

Chan (2022) extends the Bayesian VAR prior literature by keeping conjugacy while
allowing asymmetric shrinkage between own lags and other-variable lags. For
proposal writing, the references are most useful in five blocks:

- Large Bayesian VAR shrinkage: Banbura, Giannone and Reichlin (2010),
  Carriero, Clark and Marcellino (2015), Koop (2013), Korobilis and Pettenuzzo
  (2019), Schorfheide and Song (2015).
- Conjugate and nonconjugate BVAR computation: Kadiyala and Karlsson (1997),
  Sims and Zha (1998), Carriero, Clark and Marcellino (2019), Chan (2020a),
  Chan and Jacobi and Zhu (2020).
- Prior selection and marginal likelihood: Giannone, Lenza and Primiceri
  (2015), Chan, Jacobi and Zhu (2020), Zellner (1971).
- Structural VAR identification: Baumeister and Hamilton (2015), Rubio-Ramirez,
  Waggoner and Zha (2010), Furlanetto, Ravazzolo and Sarferaz (2019), Gambetti
  (2021).
- DSGE/theory-informed priors and macro interpretation: Del Negro and
  Schorfheide (2004), Canova and Paustian (2011), Christiano, Motto and
  Rostagno (2014), Loria, Matthes and Wang (2020).
