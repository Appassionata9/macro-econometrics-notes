# Koop and Korobilis (2010) References

## How to Use This List

Koop and Korobilis (2010) is a survey-style methods paper, so its references are
best read as a map of Bayesian empirical macroeconomics rather than as one
narrow empirical lineage.

For proposal writing, the most useful blocks are:

- Standard Bayesian VAR priors: Litterman (1986), Doan, Litterman and Sims
  (1984), Kadiyala and Karlsson (1997), Sims and Zha (1998), Banbura, Giannone
  and Reichlin (2008/2010).
- DSGE and DSGE-VAR links: Del Negro and Schorfheide (2004, 2009), An and
  Schorfheide (2007), Lubik and Schorfheide (2004), Fernandez-Villaverde
  (2009).
- Time-varying parameter VARs and state-space methods: Cogley and Sargent
  (2001, 2005), Primiceri (2005), Carter and Kohn (1994), Durbin and Koopman
  (2001, 2002), Fruhwirth-Schnatter (1994).
- Factor models and FAVARs: Geweke (1977), Forni and Reichlin (1998), Stock
  and Watson (2002, 2005, 2006), Bernanke and Boivin (2003), Bernanke, Boivin
  and Eliasz (2005), Belviso and Milani (2006).
- Stochastic volatility and nonlinear/state-switching extensions: Kim,
  Shephard and Chib (1998), Chib, Nardari and Shephard (2002, 2006), Pitt and
  Shephard (1999), Kim and Nelson (1999), Koop and Potter (2006, 2009).
