# Paccagnini (2010) References

## How to Use This List

This paper sits at the intersection of DSGE model validation, Bayesian
calibration, dummy-observation priors, and VAR-based empirical macroeconomics.
For proposal writing, the references are most useful in five blocks:

- DSGE-VAR and Bayesian model evaluation: Del Negro and Schorfheide (2004,
  2006), Del Negro et al. (2007a, 2007b), Schorfheide (2000), Paccagnini's
  related work with Consolo and Favero.
- Dummy-observation and Bayesian VAR priors: Theil and Goldberg (1961), Doan,
  Litterman and Sims (1984), Sims (2005), Litterman (1981).
- DSGE/RBC calibration and evaluation: Canova (1994, 1995, 2005), DeJong,
  Ingram and Whiteman (1996, 2000), Watson (1993), Gregory and Smith (1991).
- New Keynesian and monetary policy structure: Clarida, Gali and Gertler
  (2000), King (2000), Smets and Wouters (2003), Woodford (2003), Rudebusch
  and Svensson (1998), Svensson (1997).
- VAR critique and identification: Sims (1980), Chari, Kehoe and McGrattan
  (2005), Christiano, Eichenbaum and Vigfusson (2006), Fernandez-Villaverde et
  al. (2007), Ravenna (2007).
